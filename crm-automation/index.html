The CRM data crisis

91% of CRM data is incomplete, outdated, or duplicated. Sales reps spend 5.5 hours per week on manual data entry. 30% of leads are never entered into the CRM at all. And the bigger your pipeline, the worse it gets. Stale contact info, missing company details, inconsistent tagging — these aren't minor inconveniences. They're revenue killers. A contact with a wrong phone number is a dead opportunity. A deal without next steps is a deal that won't close.

Why traditional CRM management fails

Most companies rely on sales reps to log calls, update deal stages, and add notes. It's an honor system — and it doesn't work. Reps optimize for selling, not data hygiene. Managers build forecasts on data they know is wrong. Marketing segments audiences based on incomplete profiles. And when a rep leaves, their institutional knowledge walks out the door with them.

The average company changes CRM processes three times before finding something that sticks. Each change creates migration chaos. Each new tool adds another disconnected data silo. Meanwhile, the actual customer data decays at 2-3% per month — meaning in a year, a quarter of your database is useless.

AI agents that clean as they work

Automatic data entry

Every email, call, meeting, and SMS is automatically logged to the right contact, company, and deal record. No more "did you update the CRM?" Our AI agents parse conversation content, extract key details, and write structured updates in real-time.

Smart enrichment

Missing company size, industry, or funding status? The AI automatically enriches records from 20+ data sources — LinkedIn, Clearbit, Apollo, and more. Every contact gets a complete profile without a human ever touching it.

Duplicate detection & merge

Fuzzy matching algorithms identify duplicate contacts and companies across your entire database. Conflicts are resolved automatically using confidence scoring, or flagged for review when ambiguity is high.

Pipeline hygiene alerts

Deals stuck too long without activity get flagged. Records missing key fields trigger enrichment workflows. Stale contacts are re-verified and updated — or automatically archived if invalid.
