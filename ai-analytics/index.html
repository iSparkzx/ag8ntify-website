The reporting burden

Executives spend 40% of their time gathering data instead of acting on it. Marketing pulls numbers from 6 platforms. Sales exports pipeline reports that are stale the moment they're created. Operations tracks KPIs in spreadsheets that nobody trusts. And every month, someone spends an entire weekend building a board deck that should have been automatic.

Why business intelligence breaks

Most companies have the data. What they lack is the system to turn that data into insight at speed. Dashboards exist but they're always 24 hours behind. Alerts fire too late. And the moment someone asks a question that wasn't pre-built into a report, it's back to manual exports and pivot tables.

The cost isn't just time. It's decision quality. Slow data means slow decisions. And in competitive markets, the companies that move on yesterday's data are the ones that get left behind.

Intelligence that works in real-time

Unified data pipeline

AI agents connect to your CRM, ad platforms, financial systems, support tools, and custom databases. Data is extracted, normalized, and stored in a unified warehouse — automatically, continuously, and without human intervention.

Natural language queries

Ask questions in plain English and get instant answers. "What's our CAC by channel this quarter?" "Which customers are at risk of churning?" "How does pipeline velocity compare to last month?" The AI understands the question, queries the data, and returns a visualized answer.

Automated anomaly detection

The system watches every metric and flags unusual patterns before they become problems. A spike in bounce rate. A drop in sales velocity. An unusual refund pattern. Alerts go to the right person with context and recommended actions.

Board-ready reports

Weekly, monthly, or quarterly reports are generated and distributed automatically. Executive summaries with key highlights, trend analysis with visual charts, and forward-looking projections — all from live data, not stale exports.
