Where deals die

44% of sales reps give up after one follow-up attempt. The average closed deal requires 8 touchpoints, but most reps stop at 2. Proposals sit in inboxes for weeks because nobody sent a reminder. And when a deal does move forward, reps waste 2 hours per day just updating the CRM, scheduling meetings, and chasing internal approvals.

The sales admin tax

Salespeople should sell. But the reality is that 65% of their time goes to non-selling activities: data entry, scheduling, follow-up emails, proposal generation, deal status updates, and internal reporting. Every minute spent on admin is a minute not spent on prospects. And for high-performing reps, that tax is even higher — because they have more deals to manage.

The result is a pipeline that looks full but moves slow. Deals that should close in 30 days take 60. Revenue targets slip. And your best reps burn out from the administrative burden of the very success they created.

Every stage, automated

Prospect intelligence

Before every call, AI agents compile a complete prospect brief: company news, recent funding, LinkedIn activity, past interactions, and recommended talking points. Your reps walk into every conversation fully prepared.

Follow-up sequences

Context-aware email and SMS sequences that adapt to prospect behavior. If they opened the proposal, the next message references specific sections. If they ghosted after pricing, the system sends a value reinforcement message with ROI data.

Proposal automation

Generate personalized proposals in seconds — not hours. AI pulls relevant case studies, pricing, and terms based on deal size and industry. Proposals are sent, tracked, and followed up automatically.

Pipeline acceleration alerts

AI monitors deal velocity and flags at-risk opportunities before they stall. Reps get proactive alerts: "Deal XYZ hasn't had activity in 5 days — recommended action: send case study."
